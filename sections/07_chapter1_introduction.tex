\chapter{Introduction}

\section{Background}
Elections are a critical democratic process, but traditional and many electronic voting systems still face challenges in security, auditability, and accessibility. Existing systems often focus on vote collection and counting while giving limited attention to transparent audit trails, tamper awareness, inclusive voter journeys, and deployment in constrained environments.

The project \textbf{VoteGuard Pro} addresses this by combining a guided electronic voting workflow with encrypted vote persistence, a verifiable hash-chained audit path, optional biometric analytics, and a blockchain-ready anchoring pathway. The system is designed as a prototype for research and engineering evaluation in academic and controlled environments.

\section{Motivation}
The key motivation is to design a system that treats security and accessibility as equal constraints rather than trade-offs. The project is driven by the following practical concerns:
\begin{itemize}
\item Risk of vote tampering and weak post-election verification in opaque systems.
\item Voter trust deficit due to limited transparency of vote handling.
\item Limited support for accessible and guided digital voting experiences.
\item Need for modular, testable architecture that can evolve toward blockchain-backed immutability.
\end{itemize}

\section{Aims and Objectives}
\begin{enumerate}
\item Build a secure prototype that stores ballots in encrypted append-only form and maintains an audit chain.
\item Implement a guided voter journey: identifier entry, biometric capture, ballot selection, confirmation, and persistence.
\item Prevent duplicate casting within the session using cast-registry safeguards.
\item Maintain modular boundaries between UI, storage, security, devices, and optional blockchain adapters.
\item Evaluate the prototype for functionality, usability, accessibility, and operational robustness.
\end{enumerate}

\section{Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) Addressed}
The project aligns with the following SDGs:
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure):} by proposing resilient digital civic infrastructure for elections.
\item \textbf{SDG 10 (Reduced Inequalities):} by improving accessibility and guided flows for diverse voter groups.
\item \textbf{SDG 16 (Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions):} by improving transparency, auditability, and trust in electoral mechanisms.
\item \textbf{SDG 17 (Partnerships for the Goals):} by enabling integration of government, academic, and technical stakeholders.
\end{itemize}
