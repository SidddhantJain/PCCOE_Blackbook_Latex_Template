\begin{center}
\textbf{\fontsize{12}{14}\selectfont ABSTRACT}
\end{center}
VoteGuard Pro is a security-first and accessibility-aware electronic voting prototype that combines guided voter interaction, encrypted ballot persistence, and tamper-evident auditability. The system supports identifier intake, biometric capture, candidate selection, secure vote casting, and post-cast verification workflows. Ballots are stored in append-only encrypted ledgers, while audit events are recorded in a parallel hash chain to strengthen transparency and operational traceability. The design follows a modular architecture that isolates core use cases from device, storage, and blockchain adapters for testability and extensibility. Evaluation is performed using simulation scripts, unit and integration tests, and ledger verification routines. The prototype demonstrates practical feasibility for controlled academic environments and provides a pathway toward production hardening through stronger key management, formal verification, and permissioned blockchain anchoring.
